Delivery Policy

Service Area

Luminouse Beauty shop currently offers delivery services across all areas of Karachi, Pakistan. Our goal is to ensure that you receive your medicines and healthcare products safely and on time.

Delivery Charges

  • Order value below Rs. 5000 – Rs. 159 delivery fee
  • Order value Rs. 5000 and above – Free Delivery

Delivery Time

  • Orders placed before 5 PM will be delivered the same day (subject to product availability).
  • Orders placed after 5 PM will be delivered the next working day.
  • Delivery timings may vary during weekends, public holidays, or high-demand periods.

Additional Information

  • Please make sure your contact number and address are correct for smooth delivery.
  • Our delivery team will confirm your order by phone or WhatsApp before dispatch.
  • Customers are requested to check their orders upon receipt to ensure all items are correct.
  • Note: Items that require refrigeration (like insulin or vaccines) will be delivered under controlled conditions within Karachi only.
